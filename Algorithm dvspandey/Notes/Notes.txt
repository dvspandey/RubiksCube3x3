TOP layer: Step 1 - Create Daisy

HOLDING YOUR RUBIK’S CUBE

Begin by holding your Rubik’s Cube with the YELLOW CENTER piece on the UP (U) face.

Action-1
=========
Look at the top layer to locate the EDGE pieces that have a WHITE tile. Leave them where they are.

See example to the right and notice that at this stage it is okay if the white tile is not touching the YELLOW CENTER piece.

Action-2
=========
Look at the middle layer. Move EDGE pieces that have a WHITE tile from the MIDDLE layer into the top layer
